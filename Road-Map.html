Community

Grow the Beanion community through social media engagements by spreading the word on who we are, what our mission is and how do we accomplish our mission.

Collection

We will strive to expand the collection to accomodate creation of future versions of Beanions. 

Charity

The House of Beanions would like to help fight and end hunger in America by donating 50% of its accumulated cryptocurrency sales nnd royalties to Feeding America and No Kid Hungry. By minting a Beanion or buying it in the secondary market you are indirectly donating your hard-earned currency to charitable endeavours. We'll make sure that these monies help the neediest among us.

Future

We don't know what the future holds for the Beanion community but we will strive hard to accomplish to meet our goals. This will only be possible with the help of increasing our token holders base and making our little investment on these NFTs go a long way.

Events

We will partner with local food banks, pantries and soup kitchens to hold events to feed the homeless and the poor as a community of Beanions. All token holders will be given a Beanion shirt to commemorate the event and identify them as a Beanion. All Beanions from all over the country are invited to help run the events with their time and money. Remember: The House of Beanions is not a social club. It is a community of givers to the needy.
